How to find your user id
Currently you can only use your PSN to display your DR if you participated in the Daytona Gr 3 Time Trial and finished in the top 3000. Visit Gran Turismo and login to your account. Your user id is a 36 character combination of letters and numbers and will be displayed in the URL.

For example: gran-turismo.com/au/gt7/user/mymenu/12s4e6c8-1v3q-eq3c-f2lo-k2e4w6vc90gf/profile
